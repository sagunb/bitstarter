With the rise of mobile technology, we feel that it should do more than just checking facebook and twitter. Imagine crawling into bed before turning off the lights, reading your favourite book, and then going to sleep without ever having to leave the comfort of your bed.

Auto-light features fully functional wifi support. Simply activate wifi on your android/apple powered device and with the click of a button the lights will be turned off before you know it! No more stumbling in the dark, or fumbling with the bed sheet before going to bed. Simply tuck yourself into bed and let Auto-light do the rest.

FAQ

When is auto-light expected to be released?
Auto-light is currently a concept project. To make it a reality show us your support by spreading the word.
I have a blackberry, will Auto-light still work on my phone?
Yes! While there might not be a native app for blackberry users, Auto-light will be fully controllable by any browser, including the browser in your blackberry.
